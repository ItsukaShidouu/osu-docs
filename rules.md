## 1. No Cheating or Exploits
Using bots, cheats, hacks, scripts, or any third-party tools that give you an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.

## 2. Report Rule Violations
If you notice someone breaking the rules, report them through in-game reports or our Discord. Assisting or covering for rule breakers may result in penalties, including removal from NESS services.

## 3. Account Sharing is Forbidden
Using someone elseâ€™s account or sharing your login credentials is not allowed. Account sharing will result in serious punishment, including permanent bans.

## 4. Bug Abuse is Not Allowed
Do not abuse or take advantage of bugs. If you discover a bug, report it immediately to the developers. Fair rewards may be given for valid reports.

## 5. Language Policy
All public communication in-game and on the NESS Discord must be in Indonesian/English. You are free to use any language in private or non-public channels.

## 6. Respect Other Members
All players must treat each other with respect. Unwarranted insults, excessive toxicity, or disruptive behavior are not tolerated.

## 7. No Harassment
Harassment of any kind is not allowed. If someone asks you to stop, you must stop immediately. This rule is strictly enforced.

## 8. Zero Tolerance for Discrimination
Racism, sexism, transphobia, hate speech, or slurs of any kind are strictly prohibited.

## 9. No NSFW or Harmful Content
NSFW, offensive, or potentially harmful content (including flashing images, jumpscares, or extremely loud audio) is not allowed in avatars, profiles, or user pages on NESS.

## 10. No Impersonation
Impersonating other users or staff members is strictly forbidden and will be heavily punished.

## 11. No Unnecessary Drama
Do not create unnecessary drama. Report rule violations through proper channels. Public hackusations or discussing bans publicly is not allowed. Ban-related matters are strictly between NESS staff and the affected user.

## 12. Follow Staff Instructions
Use common sense at all times. If a staff member gives you instructions, follow them. Failure to comply may result in punishment.

## 14. Play Fair in Ranking
Score farming, intentional deranking, or abusing ranking systems in unintended ways is prohibited.

## 15. Modified Clients & Skins
Custom skins are allowed. Modified osu! clients or clients that alter gameplay behavior are not allowed unless explicitly approved by NESS staff.
